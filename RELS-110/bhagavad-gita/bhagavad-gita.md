# Bhagavad Gita
- "Song of God"
- Arjuna a kshatriya (warrior) and his chariot driver Krishna (avatar of Vishnu)

## Arjuna's dilemma
As a warrior, it is his duty to fight, but he doesn't want to kill his kinsmen

## Krishna's responses

1. Death is not final
2. Karma yoga
