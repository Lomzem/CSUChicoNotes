# Nirodha
Suffering can end!

Through:
- morality
- meditation
- wisdom

Become free of all attachments and attain nirvana (nibbana), free of samsara

# Nirvana
State where fires of attachment, aversion, and ignorance extinguish and one experiences true peace/happiness
