# Magga
Eightfold path

## Wisdom
- Right view (Dharma, Buddha's teachings)
- Right intention

Dharma is still cosmic law of universe, Buddha understands it

## Morality
- Right speech
- Right action
- Right livelihood

## Meditation
- Right effort
- Right concentration
- Right mindfulness
